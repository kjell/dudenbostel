These images and their accompanying narrative were posted on the internet for a while. They were all created by Lynn Dudenbostel, the luthier in many of the photos. Here he happens to be building a batch of two mandolins, one for Chris Thile and one for a fellow named Gary Hedricks.

I wish that I could build mandolins. I want to try it, and have been looking for good info on the web that might help me dive in. Although there are a few images missing, this is one of the best resources I've found.
